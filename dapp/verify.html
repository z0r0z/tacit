What this proves: (1) the asset id is SHA-256(etch txid ‖ vout) — it can only be this one etch; (2) the etch’s mint authority is zero — no new TAC can ever be valid; (3) the published opening verifies the on-chain Pedersen commitment to exactly 21,000,000. The Bitcoin tx comes from a public explorer but isn’t trusted: the asset id is recomputed locally and the IPFS blob is checked against its own content hash (the CID).
