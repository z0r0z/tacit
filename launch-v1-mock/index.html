Tacit protocol

A confidential layer for Bitcoin and Ethereum DeFi.

A tacit1 key controls private notes across both lanes: etched assets on Bitcoin, WELD settlement for ETH and ERC20s, bridge routes, and proof-backed mints such as cBTC and cUSD.

One receive handle

Bitcoin asset layer

TAC and future Tacit-compatible assets can be etched and transferred confidentially on Bitcoin.

WELD V1 pool

ConfidentialPool and Router let ETH and ERC20s enter notes, settle quickly, bridge to Bitcoin, and batch mint routes.

Permissionless DeFi
